About these amazing books and tales

These books are about a wonderfully strange world that consists of adventure and humanity, and is enjoyed by all ages. This unique world of magical culture and humor will definately appeal to other fans of Harry Potter just like me. There are crazy plot twists, action, and I love the characters that all have a different personality .

About Septimus Heap and other characters:
Their crazy ideas and adventures

Not only does Septimus have an exciting life filled with adventure, so does his friends and family. One of his best friends is his adoptive sister and the princess, Jenna. In each book they have another mysterious action-packed thriller that is always a matter of life or death.

In the first book they must fight DomDaniel, an evil and powerful wizard, and try to find their long lost brother, Septimus Heap. In the second book Septimus is the ExtraOrdnary wizard apprentice, his eldest brother, Simon kidnaps Jenna to avenge DomDaniel during his dark years. During this time Simon was out of control because he was mad at not getting his place as ExtraOrdinary apprentice. In the third book Septimus is stuck in another time and his family and friends must save him. In the fourth book Septimus is chosen to go on the dreaded queste and he must also find his brother who is stuck in a place where there is no time. In the fifth book him and his friends are stuck on an island that has creepy spirits and forces, they must escape. In the sixth book they have a dark fog that is taking over everybody's souls and they must stop it from continuously spreading. In the seventh book must choose whether he wants to pusue his goals in magic or physics and how he will reach this goal.

These are the books in the Septimus Heap series in order: Magyk, Flyte, Physik, Queste, Syren, Darke, Fyre.
